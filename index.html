Sysreview-ops

Multi-agent systematic review data extraction system powered by AI. Streamline your research workflow with PRISMA 2020 compliant tools.

🔬 PRISMA 2020 📚 Cochrane Standards 🤖 AI-Powered ⚡ Multi-Agent

🚀 Key Features

📝
Form Generation
Auto-generate extraction forms from your research questions with intelligent field mapping.
🔍
Data Extraction
AI-powered extraction of study data with support for single and dual reviewer modes.
⚙️
Batch Processing
Process multiple papers efficiently with automated pipeline management and progress tracking.
✅
Validation & Reconciliation
Automatic comparison of dual extractions with conflict resolution workflows.
⚖️
Risk of Bias Assessment
Structured quality assessment following Cochrane RoB 2.0 and ROBINS-I guidelines.
📊
Narrative Synthesis
Generate comprehensive synthesis reports with evidence tables and summary of findings.

⌨️ Quick Commands

Use these slash commands in Claude Code to interact with the system:

/review-ops form Generate extraction form
/review-ops extract Extract single paper
/review-ops batch Process all pending
/review-ops validate Reconcile dual extractions
/review-ops qa Risk of bias assessment
/review-ops synthesis Narrative synthesis
/review-ops audit Pipeline integrity check
/review-ops status Tracker dashboard
