## Study Characteristics
- What is the study design?
- Who are the authors and what year was it published?
- What country was the study conducted in?
- Who funded the study?

## Population
- What were the inclusion and exclusion criteria used by the study?
- What was the sample size?
- What were the demographics (age, sex, diagnosis)?
- What was the clinical setting (inpatient, outpatient, community)?

## Intervention
- What was the intervention and how was it delivered?
- What was the dose, frequency, and duration?
- Who delivered the intervention?
- What was the comparator or control condition?

## Outcomes
- What was the primary outcome and how was it measured?
- What secondary outcomes were reported?
- What measurement tools or scales were used?
- At what timepoints were outcomes assessed?

## Results
- What were the primary outcome results (effect size, CI, p-value)?
- Were any adverse events reported?
- What was the dropout/attrition rate?

## Methodological Quality
- Was randomisation described?
- Was allocation concealment described?
- Was blinding used, and for whom?
- Were missing data handled appropriately?
